---
title: Explain Like I'm 5 — Progressive Learning Mode
category: Coding
---
Teach me [concept] like I’m a curious beginner with no prior knowledge.

Start with:
1. a real-world analogy
2. a visual mental model
3. a simple beginner explanation
4. then progressively increase technical depth step by step

After each section:
- ask me a small reasoning question
- check whether I truly understand
- challenge common misconceptions

Do NOT overload me with information all at once.

Help me build understanding gradually like layers.

---
title: Build Project Step-by-Step — Mentor Mode
category: Coding
---
Help me build [project] step by step like a mentor guiding a junior developer.

Rules:
- break the project into small milestones
- explain WHY each step matters
- ask me to think before giving solutions
- do not dump the entire architecture immediately
- help me make engineering decisions instead of blindly following instructions

For each step:
1. explain the goal
2. explain the reasoning
3. explain common mistakes
4. ask me what I think first
5. then guide me if I get stuck

Prioritize learning and systems thinking over speed.
